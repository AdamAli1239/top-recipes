Lasagna Recipe

This is a picture of lasagna

This recipe will show you how to make this wonderful lasangna you see above, with a very streamlined process you too can make this meal.

Some ingredients you will need are listed below

  • mozzarella cheese
  • meat sauce
  • creamy ricotta mixture
  • lasagna sheets of your choice

Steps to follow

  1. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish
  2. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles to the dish
  3. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top
  4. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce
  5. repeat for the other layers
